Drag the blue cursor across the plot, use the Day slider, or click the plot and press the left/right arrow keys (Shift for larger steps, Home/End for the ends of the year). The curve is the declination of the full moon; the shaded band shows its range as the moon wanders above and below the ecliptic.

Horizon Diagram

The sphere shows the sky for a northern-hemisphere observer (latitude 41°) with the green horizon plane. The gold disc is the Sun and the grey disc is the full Moon, always opposite the Sun. Drag the sphere to rotate your view, or click it and use the arrow keys.
